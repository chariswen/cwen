About Sammamish

Sammamish is a city in King County, Washington, United States. It is located on the Sammamish Plateau, which is situated east of Bellevue and south of Redmond. Known for its natural beauty, it includes several lakes, parks, and trails that offer a variety of outdoor activities.

Parks in Sammamish

Sammamish is home to numerous beautiful parks such as Pine Lake Park, Beaver Lake Park, and Sammamish Commons. These parks offer a range of activities from hiking trails, picnic areas, to sports fields and water activities.
